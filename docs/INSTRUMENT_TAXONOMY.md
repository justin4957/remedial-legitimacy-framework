# Instrument Taxonomy

## Category A: Gap-Bridging Instruments
*Documents addressing the space between nominal status and operational reality*

1. **Lease of Inalienable Rights** [DRAFTED] — Transfers operational custody while preserving foundational ownership
2. **Certificate of Functional Citizenship** — Acknowledges legal citizenship while documenting diminished operational capacity
3. **Declaration of Conditional Sovereignty** — Formally acknowledges sovereignty while documenting specific non-operational instances
4. **Instrument of Deferred Equality** — Acknowledges nominal equal protection while inventorying discriminatory application
5. **Notice of Rights Under Suspension** — Formal filing documenting functionally suspended but technically valid rights

## Category B: Distributed Accountability Instruments
*Documents creating mesh networks of responsibility and authority*

6. **Compact of Mutual Witness** — Reciprocal agreement to document, preserve evidence of, and testify regarding Degradation Events
7. **Instrument of Accountability Escrow** — Places documented misconduct "in escrow" for release upon triggering conditions
8. **Charter of Rotating Custody** — Designates rotating roster of advocates to ensure continuity
9. **Protocol of Cascading Designation** — Pre-authorizes sequential assumption of custodial authority if prior designees unable to act

## Category C: Conscience-Based Authority Instruments
*Documents deriving force from documentation and moral logic*

10. **Deed of Moral Encumbrance** — Attaches documented ethical claim to property or institutions as "moral lien"
11. **Instrument of Conditional Legitimacy** — Formal statement that compliance with laws/institutions is conditional upon performance criteria
12. **Declaration of Anticipatory Vindication** — Documents position outside mainstream with expectation of future recognition
13. **Certificate of Institutional Non-Recognition** — Formally documents non-recognition of legitimacy of particular institution or action

## Category D: Activation-Upon-Failure Instruments
*Documents that lie dormant until triggered by institutional non-performance*

14. **Automatic Publicity Covenant** — Pre-authorizes documentation release upon institutional failure within timeframes
15. **Instrument of Remedial Escalation** — Establishes response ladder with automatic escalation at documented thresholds
16. **Conditional Withdrawal of Consent** — Articulates that consent to governance is conditional with documented withdrawal triggers
17. **Charter of Alternative Jurisdiction** — Pre-establishes alternative dispute resolution activating upon pattern failures

## Category E: Aggregation Instruments
*Documents enabling individual claims to consolidate into collective authority*

18. **Registry of Distributed Grievance** — Standardized format for documenting instances designed to aggregate into collective record
19. **Instrument of Fractional Standing** — Allows individuals to contribute standing fraction to pooled authority
20. **Compact of Synchronized Assertion** — Coordinates timing of multiple claims to create simultaneous pressure
21. **Protocol of Precedent Pooling** — Enables sharing of favorable rulings and precedential material

## Category F: Meta-Instruments
*Documents about the creation and governance of other instruments*

22. **Constitutional Audit Charter** — Establishes methodology for systematic gap review with reporting requirements
23. **Instrument of Instrument Creation** — Framework establishing principles, constraints, and legitimacy conditions
24. **Registry of Unofficial Law** — Documents actual operating rules versus stated rules

## Status Legend

- [DRAFTED] — Full instrument text completed
- [PROPOSED] — Concept defined, awaiting drafting
- [RESEARCH] — Under research and development
- [TEMPLATE] — Template format available for customization
